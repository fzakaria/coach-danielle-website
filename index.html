My Story

From The Pool To The Box

I grew up in the water, swimming and playing water polo through high school, eventually earning a scholarship to play at the University of Hawaii. It was there, during my senior year off-season, that I discovered CrossFit under founders Greg and Lauren Glassman.

That discovery changed everything. After college, I became the strength and conditioning coach for my water polo team, helping lead them to the NCAA Final 4.

Today, as an owner at CrossFit Santa Cruz, I am passionate about using functional movements to build health. I believe in support, enthusiasm, and the power of mental toughness to help you achieve your goals.
